# MacCrab v1.21.0-rc.7

## Overview dashboard — Crabby, the EDR companion

Crabby now lives in a little world that reflects your Mac's live security state:

- **Weather sky** — sun when clear, clouds on alerts, a storm with lightning when alarmed.
- **Event terrain** — a tiny hill of your recent event volume that he stands on; he gets livelier as the event rate climbs.
- **Mood beacon** — an antenna bulb over his head (green / orange / red).
- **AI sidekick** — a little robot joins him when an AI agent is active.
- **Coverage shield** — a shield appears when every sensor is healthy.
- **Offline state** — he goes greyscale and asleep if the engine isn't running.
- **Ambient life** — idle blinks, a gentle breathing sway, and a "snap" when a new alert lands.
- **Interactions** — Pet him (hearts), Feed him (a crumb he snaps — refreshes threat intel), Acknowledge an alarm, or Investigate. Reduce Motion freezes the animation.
